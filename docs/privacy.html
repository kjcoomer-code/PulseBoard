PulseBoard Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PulseBoard collects, uses, stores, and deletes information when you use the PulseBoard app and related website.

Information PulseBoard processes

PulseBoard may process workout information that you import manually and Oura data that you authorize through OAuth, including daily readiness, sleep score, activity score, heart rate variability, resting heart rate, and steps.

How information is used

PulseBoard uses this information to display fitness progress, recovery trends, training balance, and related summaries inside the app.

Storage

In the current prototype, Oura access and refresh tokens are stored locally on the device running the app. Imported workout data and synced Oura data are also used locally unless you add your own hosting or backend storage later.

Sharing

PulseBoard does not sell personal information. PulseBoard does not share Oura data with third parties except as necessary to make authorized requests to Oura’s API.

User control and deletion

You can disconnect Oura inside the app, which removes the locally stored token cache used by the prototype. You can also delete local app files from your device to remove imported or synced data.

Security

PulseBoard uses a server-side OAuth flow so Oura client secrets are not exposed in the browser. Local tokens are stored using operating-system level secure string protection available to the current user profile.

Children

PulseBoard is not directed to children under 13.

Medical disclaimer

PulseBoard is not medical advice and is not intended for diagnosis or treatment.
