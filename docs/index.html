What PulseBoard does

Imports Apple workout data, connects to Oura through OAuth, and creates a unified dashboard for training strain and recovery balance.

Who it is for

Individuals who want a simpler view of whether their training volume and recovery patterns are moving in the right direction.

Data used

PulseBoard may use Oura daily metrics such as readiness, sleep, HRV, resting heart rate, activity score, and steps, plus workout data supplied by the user.

Important note

PulseBoard is a fitness tracking tool. It is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and should not be used to diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical condition.
